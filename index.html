Best Tourist Spots in the Philippines

Take a moment to appreciate the beauty of our country. Listed below are
some of the best places you could take your feet and appreciate the beauty of our country, the Philippines.


Calle Crisologo

Calle Crisologo features an old-world charm and Spanish colonial architecture. It was also able to maintain the history district, therefore calesas or horse-drawn carriages are still available. As you roam around this place, you could feel like you have traveled back in time.

Mayon Volcano

The Mayon Volcano is the world’s most perfect volcanic cone and most active volcano in the Philippines. Mayon comes from the Bicolano word “magayon”, meaning beautiful. Mayon is extremely beautiful, despite the past eruptions that affected several towns.

Manila Cathedral

Manila Cathedral features a Romanesque Revival of Neo-Romanesque style. This church is one of the most significant and magnificent churches built during the Spanish colonization.

Kayangan Lake

Kayangan Lake is one of the most photographed and iconic destinations in the Philippines. This features its lagoon that is a dreamy mix of blues and greens glistening on a bright, sunny day.

Malapascua Island

Malapascua Island is known for being a diving destination where you can swim with thresher sharks. Its small island culture, smaller crowds, and the long stretch of white-sand beaches attract tourists the most.

Cloud 9

Located in Siargao, Cloud 9 is the most famous surfing spot in the entire country and a must in any tourist’s Siargao itinerary. It is known for its thick, hollow tubes and fast-barreling right-hand waves that break over a reef.

Las Casas Filipinas De Acuzar

Located in Bataan, Las Casas Filipinas de Acuzar is the first-ever heritage resort in the country. It is one of the most unique hotels in the Philippines. It showcases over 30 heritage homes from different parts of the Philippines fully-restored brick-by-brick to their glory days.

Tagaytay Picnic Grove

Located in Tagaytay, Tagaytay Picnic Grove is a park that consists of entertaining activities such as eco-trail, horseback riding, zipline, cable rides. The breathtaking view and extremely cold weather attracts tourists the most.

White Beach

Located in Boracay, White Beach is one of the best white sand beaches in the Philippines and in the entire world. Tall coconut trees and crystal-clear calm waters is the reason for its unmatched beauty. The view during sunsets and evening attracts tourists the most.
